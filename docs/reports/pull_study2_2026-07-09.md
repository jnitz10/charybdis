# Study-2 cheap-data pull — 2026-07-09

## Safety and meter pricing

All CoinAPI objects were listed and sized before download. Trades and quotes were
listed from exact hourly L4 exchange prefixes. CoinAPI's LIST response omitted the
exchange-wide HLSYSTEMEVENTS object from `Contents`, so those known exact keys were
sized with free HEAD requests before any GET. Requests used at most four workers,
a shared 150-RPM limiter, and exponential backoff for 403/429 responses. No
backoff was needed. Data occupies 12 GB, below the 25 GB limit.

`T-HLORACLEPRICES`, `T-HLSYSTEMEVENTS`, and `T-HLTWAPSTATUSES` are distinct meter
SKUs priced conservatively at the Trades tiers ($24/$12/$6 per decimal GB). They
are explicitly present in `ASSUMED_PRICE_SKUS`; this is an over-estimating pricing
assumption, not a verified CoinAPI rate.

## Free funding history

Hyperliquid REST enumeration found nine HIP-3 dexes and 225 markets. All 225 were
queried with full-history pagination (at most 500 rows/call and at most one
request/second); 180 markets returned data, 45 metadata markets had empty
histories, and 598,282 unique market-hour rows were written to
`data/study2_funding.parquet`. The five required series contain: SKHX 3,372,
SMSN 3,371, SMH 592, KR200 2,218, and EWY 3,117 rows.

Within every non-empty series, adjacent observations had zero gaps strictly
greater than two hours. Empty histories have no bounded interval on which to
assert continuity and are listed separately in `data/study2_funding_meta.json`.
